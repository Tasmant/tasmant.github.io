About this site

I read a lot and forget most of it. This is my attempt to keep track — not as a literary critic, just as someone who likes books and wants to remember what they thought about them.

Reviews are short and honest. I rate books out of five stars, though I find ratings a bit silly and mostly use them as a quick shorthand.

No newsletter, no comments, no ads. Just a list of books.
